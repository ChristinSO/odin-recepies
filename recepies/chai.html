Masala Chai(Tea) Recepie

Let's get this Par-tea started!

Let's start off with the ingredients:

  • Black tea
  • Whole Spices
  • Milk
  • Sweetner

Here are spices you need:

  • Fresh Ginger
  • Cardamom
  • Cinnamon
  • Cloves
  • Peppercorns
  • Star anise

Now follow these instructions to make some chai:

  1. Crush spices and add them to a pot with 1 cup of water.
  2. Add tea and bring to boil.
  3. Turn off the heat and let it settele for 10 minutes.
  4. Add milk and stir.
  5. Add sweetner as needed
  6. Enjoy your Quali-tea.

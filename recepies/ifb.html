Indian Flat Bread Recipe

Let's start off with the ingredients:

  • 1 Cup whole wheat flour
  • 1 Cup AP flour
  • 1 Teaspoon salt/li>
  • 2 Tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 Cup water or as needed

Now follow these instructions to make some roti...ahem, I mean Flatbread:

  1. In a large bowl, stir together the whole wheat flour, all-purpose flour and salt.
  2. Use a wooden spoon to stir in the olive oil and enough water to make a soft dough that is elastic but not sticky.
  3. Knead the dough on a lightly floured surface until it is smooth.
  4. Divide into 10 parts, or less if you want bigger breads.
  5. Roll each piece into a ball. Let rest for a few minutes.
  6. Heat a skillet over medium heat until hot, and grease lightly.
  7. On a lightly floured surface, use a floured rolling pin to roll out the balls of dough until very thin.
  8. When the pan starts smoking, put a chapati on it. Cook until the underside has brown spots, about 30 seconds, then flip and cook on the other side.
  9. Do the same with the remaining dough and serve.
